    Have a question? Check out the FAQ

    At PeakPoint Records, we guide you through every step of your world-record journey—combining personalized coaching, strategic planning, and expert support to turn your boldest ambitions into officially recognized Guinness World Records.

    What does PeakPoint Records do?

    PeakPoint Records helps individuals and organizations plan, prepare, and attempt Guinness World Records (GWR) by offering strategic guidance, documentation assistance, application support, and post-attempt coordination.

    Can anyone apply for a world record?

    Yes! Anyone can attempt a world record as long as they meet the specific guidelines set by Guinness World Records. We help you identify suitable categories and ensure your attempt meets all requirements.

    What types of records can I attempt?

    There are thousands of categories, including physical feats, group events, endurance challenges, artistic performances, and unique talents. We help you find or create a category that fits your strengths or vision.

    How long does it take to get a record approved?

    It typically takes 8–12 weeks for Guinness to review an application. With a priority application (extra fee), the review time can be reduced to 5 working days. We help you choose the right path based on your goals.

    What if my record attempt fails?

    Not all attempts succeed on the first try. We offer post-attempt analysis, suggest improvements, and support you through re-application and re-attempts if needed..

    How much does it cost to work with PeakPoint Records?

    Our packages vary based on the type of record, level of support, and urgency. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your idea and timeline..

    Can PeakPoint Records guarantee a world record?

    While we can’t guarantee success, we maximize your chances by thoroughly preparing you, handling the paperwork, and making sure your attempt aligns perfectly with official guidelines.

    Do you help with media coverage or promotions?

    Yes! We offer add-on services for branding, social media campaigns, press releases, and event management to boost your record’s visibility.
